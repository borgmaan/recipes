# Cinnamon Swirl Bread

### Ingredients

#### Bread

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tsp baking power
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup buttermilk -- can sub w/ milk & tbsp vinegar 
- 1/4 cup oil
- 2 eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

#### Toppings

- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tsp margarine

***************

### Instructions

1. Mix ingredients
2. Pour in greased bread pan
3. Drizzle topping mixture and swirl
4. Bake for 50 mins @ 350 degrees
